Industry Partnerships in Frederick County

All Platforms

Did you know? Career and Technical Education (CTE) programs are shaped by real workforce needs through required Local Advisory Committees (LACs) - a key component of the Perkins V legislation. In Frederick County, educators and local business and industry leaders come together to share insights that ensure CTE programs align with local employment demands and students gain in-demand skills, access apprenticeships, and graduate ready for meaningful college and career pathways right here at home.
