How to Use This Form

Option 1: Share Directly to Social Media
  1. Fill out the form below
  2. Click "Generate Post"
  3. Click the Social Copy tab
  4. Click "Copy to Clipboard"
  5. Open Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, or Instagram
  6. Paste and post! (Add image separately if needed)
Option 2: Add to the Toolkit
  1. Fill out the form and generate post
  2. Save your image to the images/ folder
  3. Copy the HTML and JavaScript outputs
  4. Add to index.html (or email to web team)
  5. Post is now available for everyone to share!
